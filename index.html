Welcome to Sunny Hill Festival in Prishtina

An international music festival of the highest standards, Sunny Hill brings together over 100,000 music lovers from all over the world in every edition to celebrate unforgettable performances, incredible energy, and a vibrant community.

Highlights

Background

Sunny Hill Festival is the biggest music festival in Prishtina / Kosovo and, based on its lineup of globally known headliners, arguably the largest in Southeast Europe. Founded by Dua Lipa and his family, the festival is twice nominated as one of the Top 10 Best Mid-Sized Festivals of Europe.

Gallery

Previous Headliners

With past headliners like Dua Lipa, Miley Cyrus, Calvin Harris, J Balvin, Stormzy, Skepta, Bebe Rexha, Burna Boy, Diplo, Black Coffee, Martinez Brothers, Groove Armada, Afrojack, Hardwell, The Blaze and many others – the festival continues to grow in scale, impact, and experience.

Purpose

Throughout the years, the festival has placed Prishtina on the international festival map — a must-visit cultural destination in a country that loves music and knows how to have fun.
